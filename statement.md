# Personal Indian Meal Planner - STATEMENT.md

## Problem Statement
Many individuals struggle to maintain healthy eating habits that align with their fitness goals and dietary restrictions. The challenge is particularly pronounced for those who enjoy Indian cuisine but need to balance traditional dishes with specific nutritional requirements. People often face difficulties in:

- Finding meals that match their fitness objectives (weight loss, muscle gain, or maintenance)
- Identifying dishes that accommodate their food allergies and dietary preferences
- Understanding the nutritional content of different meal options
- Balancing cultural food preferences with health goals
- Quickly accessing personalized meal recommendations without extensive research

## Scope of the Project
The Personal Indian Meal Planner addresses these challenges by providing:

*In-Scope:
- Curated database of Indian and international meals with complete nutritional information
- Goal-based filtering (weight loss, muscle gain, maintenance)
- Allergy-aware meal recommendations
- Dietary preference accommodation (vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free)
- Command-line interface for easy interaction
- Real-time meal filtering and suggestion generation

**Out-of-Scope:**
- Meal preparation instructions or recipes
- Grocery list generation
- Calorie tracking over time
- User profile storage and history
- Mobile application interface
- Integration with fitness trackers or health apps
- Meal scheduling or planning across multiple days

## Target Users
**Primary Users:**
- Health-conscious individuals following Indian cuisine
- People with specific fitness goals (weight loss, muscle building, maintenance)
- Individuals with food allergies or dietary restrictions
- Those seeking to incorporate traditional Indian meals into a healthy lifestyle

**Secondary Users:**
- Fitness enthusiasts looking for diverse meal options
- People transitioning to specific diets (vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free)
- Busy professionals needing quick, healthy meal ideas
- International food enthusiasts interested in Indian cuisine

## High-level Features
1. **Intelligent Meal Filtering System**
   - Goal-oriented meal selection based on calorie and nutrient requirements
   - Dynamic filtering for weight loss (≤400 calories, ≤15g fat)
   - Muscle gain optimization (≥30g protein, ≥350 calories)
   - Flexible maintenance mode with no restrictions

2. **Comprehensive Allergy Management**
   - Support for common allergens: nuts, gluten, dairy, eggs, fish
   - Automatic exclusion of problematic ingredients
   - Multi-allergy support through comma-separated input

3. **Dietary Preference Accommodation**
   - Vegan and vegetarian meal identification
   - Gluten-free option filtering
   - Flexible preference system for future expansion

4. **Nutritional Transparency**
   - Complete macronutrient breakdown for each meal
   - Calorie information display
   - Protein, carbohydrate, and fat content visibility

5. **User-Friendly Interaction**
   - Simple command-line interface
   - Clear input prompts and instructions
   - Human-readable meal descriptions and nutritional data
   - Informative error handling for no-match scenarios

6. **Cultural Integration**
   - Blend of traditional Indian dishes and international options
   - Authentic meal names with nutritional adaptations
   - Recognition of dietary patterns common in Indian cuisine
